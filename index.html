Things I like

Manga/Anime

Attack on Titan (進撃の巨人) ended in April 2021 with somewhat controversial ending. The final season of anime will come out this fall. The drastic change in settings astonished me and made it one of the best mangas ever. This manga successfuly depicts issues we have been facing and has us think of those problems.

One Piece (ワンピース) is definitely one of my favorite mangas. This manga not only fulfills our desire for adventure but also moves readers' heart. Even though there are more than 100 volumes, this is the first to recommend to my friends!

Space Brothers (宇宙兄弟) is a feel-good manga where brothers are trying to be astronauts. Even though this anime is not as famous as those mentioned above, Space Brothers makes you both laugh and cry with no doubt.
